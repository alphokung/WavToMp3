Welcome to the Neko Converter support center. We're here to help you get the most out of your audio conversion experience cleanly, securely, and offline.

How to Use The App

  1. Launch the Neko Converter app on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Tap the "Meow Wav File to Convert" button.
  3. Select a valid .wav file from your iCloud Drive or local Files app.
  4. Wait briefly for the offline conversion engine to compress your audio.
  5. Once complete, tap "Export MP3" to share, email, or save the MP3 output.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Why won't my file select? Neko Converter currently only supports uncompressed .wav file formats. Please ensure your file ends in ".wav" beforehand.
2. Do I need an internet connection? No! All audio conversions are performed 100% locally on your device for absolute privacy and maximum speed.
3. Where did my converted MP3 go? After conversion, you must tap the export button to manually save the MP3 into your Files app or share it to another app. It will not appear in Apple Music automatically.
