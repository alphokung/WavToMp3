Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

Welcome to Neko Converter. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your personal information and how we handle the data within our application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Short Version: We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share your audio files, voice recordings, or any personal data.

Neko Converter is designed to be a fully offline utility application. All audio conversions (from WAV to MP3) occur strictly locally on your own Apple device. At no point do your audio files leave your device or get uploaded to any external servers or cloud services.

2. Offline Processing

We believe in privacy by design. Because the core functionality of Neko Converter relies on the processing power of your own device, an internet connection is not required to convert your files. Consequently, it is physically impossible for us to access, listen to, or collect your sensitive audio recordings.

3. Analytics and Tracking

We do not integrate any third-party tracking software or analytics tools that monitor your usage of the app. We do not track your behavior, and we do not serve personalized advertisements.

4. Children's Privacy

Our application is completely safe for all audiences, including children. Since we do not collect any personal data, we inadvertently comply with all global children's privacy protection laws (such as COPPA and GDPR-K).

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. However, our commitment to zero data collection regarding your audio files will never change. Any updates to this policy will be reflected on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or the Neko Converter app, please feel free to reach out to us at our official contact channels.
